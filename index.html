About Pauline Pierson

Pauline Pierson is a Licensed Massage Therapist with 21 years of professional experience. Recently trained and certified in Advanced Muscle Reconstruction (ARM)—a specialized myofascial technique created by Jimmy Bluff—Pauline specializes in addressing chronic pain, postural imbalances, and movement restrictions at their source.

While Pauline started her massage therapy career later in life, helping people get out of pain has been her calling since high school. For more than 21 years, she has dedicated her practice to understanding the root causes of her clients' discomfort rather than simply treating symptoms. Her passion for learning led her to study Advanced Muscle Reconstruction, allowing her to provide more effective and lasting results.

At Advanced Bodywork with Pauline, her goal is to partner with you on your healing journey. Together, you can create positive change and help you return to the activities you love. In her spare time, she can be seen in-line skating or partner dancing to big band swing music.

What is Advanced Muscle Reconstruction (ARM)?
Advanced Muscle Reconstruction is a specialized myofascial technique created by Jimmy Bluff, designed to restore mobility, eliminate pain, and repair muscle dysfunction without the force, bruising, and inflammation typical to other modalities. Through precise, gentle work, ARM releases deep adhesions, reactivates blood flow, and realigns tissue for lasting results. ARM is ideal for chronic pain sufferers and even those with complex conditions like scoliosis or nerve entrapment. It's for anyone who has done everything else and still hasn't found relief.
